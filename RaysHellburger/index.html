Who We Are


Serving Great Burgers since 1967

Ray's Hellburger is an incredible local burger joint that opened its doors only a year after our founder discovered the secret to creating a flawless burger. These delicious burgers are nothing like what you have tasted before. Cooked to perfection and with just the right amount of added kick, you'll know exactly why Ray decided to call them the Hellburger.

We've carried the soul of our excellent original Hellburger into everything we make. From fries, to hot dogs and sandwiches, we have something for everyone. Our cuisine may seem familiar, but we gaurantee that it will be like nothing you have ever tasted before.
