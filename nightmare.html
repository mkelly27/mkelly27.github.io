Nightmare

In this Skyrim mod, I attempted to create a horror experience that is unlike what a player would normally experience within Skyrim. The level is hub based and seamlessly moves the player throughout with no backtracking, as shown in the pre-cluttered and pre-lit screenshot. The blended cave and castle tileset make the castle look as though it was in ruin. Each room attempts to tell a story of those that live there, and even the story of the adventurers that wandered through this cast after it had been long abandoned.

As the name implies, this level is designed for a mod in which the player is having a nightmare, meaning that various assets can be used to create a sense of horror to the player, and multiple objects (such as gargoyles) can move throughout the level based off of what the player is currently doing.

Role: Level Design
