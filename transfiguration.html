Transfiguration

Transfiguration is a shapeshifting and gravity shifting platforming game that rewards quick thinking as well as reaction time. For the development of this game, I wanted to challenge myself to create an appealing and unique game that was also minimalist in its aesthetics. The mechanics of each shape are simple, but the player can combo them together to in engaging ways.

I was responsible primarily for programming and gameplay design, but also created some of the levels.

Team Size: 2

Role: Gameplay Designer, Programmer, Level Designer
