Federyc

Federyc's centered around creating a dark fantasy atmosphere within a first person perspective. In order to sell this mood and the story that goes along with it, I opted for a slower paced experience, rather than a quick and action packed one. This comes across in the level, as well as game through the games combat.

As Aeylith, you explore the dungeons created by the local noble lord named Federyc. People have been vanishing recently, and you have to try to find a way to escape.

Team Size: 1

Target Platform: PC

Role: Gameplay Designer, Level Designer, Programmer, Writing
