FireBound

In FireBound, the player finds themself in a pocket dimension housed within a lantern. They must fight 3 bosses to escape from the dimension.
Through their journey, the player must engage in first person melee combat in which they use their time slowing powers and incredible strength to battle countless lost souls.
The player can rely solely on their fade ability to evade attacks, or can engage in some aerial combat to juggle their opponents.

I was responsible for the initial design of the game, as well as bringing the character controller and enemies to life by coding various iterations of their behaviors using UE4 blueprints and behavior trees.

Role: Game Design, Enemy programming, Character Programming
