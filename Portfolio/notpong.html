Not Pong

Not Pong was made as a challenge to myself. I wanted to see if I could take a simple idea like Pong, and expand it to encorporate new ideas because of a computer antogonist. The idea could be expanded upon much more heavily, but this prototype allows you to see the tone!

This was a solo project, everything aside from free sound clips were made by me.
