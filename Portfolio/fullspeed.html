Full Speed

Full Speed is an endless runner mobile game that focuses on speed. In full speed, the player is either moving, or shooting. Since shooting is the only way to slow down, the player must manage their energy to destroy their opponent, while trying to make sure they don't crash into anything!

This was a solo project, so all design and programming is mine. Royalty free sprites and sounds were found online
