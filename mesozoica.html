Mesozoica

Mesozoica is a recently released dinosaur park management game in which players plan and manage dinosaur parks. I came onto the team as a Junior Designer, and have been working with various systems that are both in the game for launch, or are coming very soon.

Some of the systems that I have designed include the dinosaur breeding system, aging system, and the quest system
