Mesozoica

Mesozoica is a currently unreleased dinosaur park management game in which players plan and manage dinosaur parks. I came onto the team as a Junior Designer, and have been working with various systems that will be either going into the game before launch, or coming shortly thereafter.

The current systems I am designing include the dinosaur breeding system, aging system, and the quest system

Role: Junior Systems Designer
